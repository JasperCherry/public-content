Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb 3 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this mobile application (the "App") handles user
information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal or real-world user data.

The only data stored by the App is:

An optional nickname chosen freely by the user
(not a real name and not used to identify a real person)

Game progress data, such as:

Riddles completed

Progress or achievements within the App

This data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the App.

2. Personal Information

We do not collect:

Real names

Email addresses

Phone numbers

Location data

Device identifiers

Contacts

Photos or media

Any other personally identifiable information

The App can be used anonymously.

3. How Data Is Used

The limited data stored by the App is used only to:

Save user progress

Improve the gameplay experience

Allow users to continue where they left off

The data is not used for advertising, tracking, profiling, or analytics.

4. Data Storage

All stored data consists only of game-related progress and the optional nickname.
This data is stored locally on the device or in the App’s backend (if applicable) and
is not linked to any real-world identity.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties.

No data is transmitted to advertisers, analytics providers, or external services.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children or adults.
Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe to use without providing
identifiable information.

7. User Control

Users may reset or remove their game progress at any time by clearing the App data
or uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected
by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
