# Privacy Policy for Forteye

**Last updated: 29 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **[YOUR COMPANY / LEGAL NAME]** ("Forteye", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Forteye mobile application (the "App").

Forteye is a security tool that helps people confirm that the person behind a sensitive request is genuinely who they claim to be, before an action is approved. To do this, the App necessarily processes some personal data. We keep this to the minimum needed to run the service, and we do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

We only collect information needed to operate the verification service.

**Information you provide:**

- **Account details** — your name and email address, used to create your account and sign you in.
- **Verification factors** — a personal PIN and/or an authenticator (TOTP) setup that you create to prove your identity. Your PIN is used to verify you; we do not store it in plain text.
- **Recovery information** — backup/recovery codes generated for you so you can regain access if you lose a verification factor.
- **Verification requests and responses** — the requests you create or are asked to approve, including titles, descriptions, content, attached records or files, and your decision to approve or reject. Sensitive request content is encrypted and only revealed to verified users.
- **Organisation/group membership** — the accounts, organisations, or groups you belong to, your role within them, and invitations you send or receive.

**Information collected automatically:**

- **Authentication tokens** — secure session tokens issued when you sign in and when you complete verification. These are stored securely on your device (see "Data Storage and Security").
- **Basic technical data** — standard information needed to communicate with our servers (such as your device's request to our API). We do **not** use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software in the App.

**Information we do NOT collect:**

- We do not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or files on your device.
- We do not use advertising identifiers or behavioural tracking.
- We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account and sign you in securely.
- Verify your identity using the verification factors you set up (email code, authenticator app, PIN).
- Create, send, and process verification requests, and record approvals, rejections, and timestamps for an audit trail.
- Notify you about requests that need your attention (for example, by email).
- Let you manage organisations or groups, members, roles, and invitations.
- Help you recover access to your account.
- Keep the service secure and prevent fraud and abuse.

## Clipboard Access

The App uses your clipboard only when you actively choose to copy information (such as a recovery code) or paste content (such as an invitation or verification link). We do not read your clipboard in the background.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

- **On your device:** session tokens are stored in the device's secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain). Cached display data (such as your name and current organisation) is stored in the App's private storage and is cleared when you sign out.
- **On our servers:** your data is processed and stored by our backend service, hosted with reputable cloud providers. Sensitive verification request content is encrypted.
- All communication between the App and our servers uses encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information using appropriate technical and organisational measures.

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in these limited cases:

- **With other users in your organisation or group**, as required for the verification service to work — for example, the people involved in a request can see who created it and who approved or rejected it.
- **With service providers** who host and operate our infrastructure on our behalf, under appropriate confidentiality and data-protection obligations.
- **When required by law**, such as to comply with a legal obligation, court order, or valid government request, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

## Data Retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service and maintain verification audit records. When you delete your account, or when data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymise it, except where we are required to retain it to comply with legal obligations.

- Correct inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your data.

- Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.
