PRIVACY POLICY FOR MOTION ALARM
Effective Date: 13 April 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
   Motion Alarm ("the App") provides motion-based alarm functionality.
   Your privacy is important, and this Privacy Policy explains how
   information is handled when you use the App.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
   Motion Alarm does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically:

* No account registration is required
* No names, emails, or contact details are collected
* No location data is collected
* No device identifiers are collected
* No user-generated data is collected or stored

All functionality operates entirely on your device.

3. LOCAL DATA (APP SETTINGS ONLY)
   The App may store basic settings locally on your device, such as:

* Alarm configuration (e.g., sensitivity, sound preferences)

This data:

* Never leaves your device
* Is not transmitted to any servers
* Is stored only to ensure the App functions correctly

4. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION
   Since Motion Alarm does not collect or store personal data externally:

* No personal data is retained by the developer
* All app-related data exists only locally on your device

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

* Clearing the app storage via your device settings, or
* Uninstalling the App

Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all associated data.

5. DEVICE PERMISSIONS
   The App may request access to the following features:

* Motion Sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope):
  Used only to detect movement and trigger alarms

* Audio / Speaker:
  Used to play alarm sounds

* Camera / Flashlight:
  Used only for flashlight functionality during alarms
  (No photos or videos are captured)

* Screen Brightness Control:
  Used to improve visibility during alarm events

All permissions are used strictly for core functionality and never
for data collection.

6. DATA SHARING
   We do not share any data with third parties, as no data is collected.

7. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
   The App does not use:

* Analytics tools
* Advertising SDKs
* External tracking services

8. DATA SECURITY
   Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no
   risk of external data exposure.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
   The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children
   under 13. Since no data is collected, it is suitable for general
   audiences.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
    This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be
    posted with a revised Effective Date.
