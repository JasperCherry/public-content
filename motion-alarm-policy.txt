# Privacy Policy for Motion Alarm

**Effective Date:** 13 April 2026

## 1. Introduction

Motion Alarm ("the App") is designed to provide a simple motion-based alarm functionality. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

Motion Alarm does **not collect, store, or share any personal data**.

Specifically:

* We do not require account registration.
* We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.
* We do not collect location data.
* We do not collect device identifiers.
* We do not collect or store any user-generated data.

## 3. Device Permissions

The App may request access to certain device features solely to provide its functionality:

* **Motion Sensors (e.g., accelerometer, gyroscope):**
  Used only to detect movement and trigger the alarm.

* **Audio / Speaker Access:**
  Used to play the alarm sound.

* **Camera / Flashlight Access:**
  Used only to enable the device flashlight (torch) as part of the alarm or alert functionality. The App does not capture photos or record video.

* **Screen Brightness Control:**
  Used to increase screen visibility during an alarm event. No data related to screen usage is collected or stored.

These permissions are used **only locally on your device** and are never used to collect, store, or transmit data.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not share any data** with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Motion Alarm does **not use any third-party services**, analytics tools, or advertising SDKs that collect user data.

## 6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, used, or disclosed by the App.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Motion Alarm does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, the App is safe for general audiences.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
